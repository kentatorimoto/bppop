Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Overview

BP pop ("the App") is developed by Kenta Torimoto. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

BP pop does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data — including blood pressure readings and pulse records — is stored exclusively on your device.

Microphone

The App uses your device's microphone solely for voice-based blood pressure input via on-device speech recognition. Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

Third-Party Services

BP pop does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. Deleting the app will permanently remove all stored data.

Children's Privacy

BP pop does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
